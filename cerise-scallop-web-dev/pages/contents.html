Starting a project

  1. What information should I provide?

    You've to provide all the necessary source files of the design including all assets.

  2. In what format should I provide the source files?

    You can send in many file types including PSD/PDF/XD/AI/Sketch/Figma/PNG/JPG.

  3. How do I submit a site which has already been coded but needs a rework?

    The best way to do this is to .ZIP up the files and submit them.

Production

  1. Can I track progress of my project?

    Yes, you can. I'll share a live preview link for your project.

  2. Do you provide any revisions?

    Yes, I'll give you revisions untill you're satisfied with the result.

  3. Do you charge for revisions?

    I do not charge for any bugs you may find which are inside the scope of the original order. If something falls outside my scope and if I feel it needs an additional charge I will let you know beforehand so there are no surprises.

Delivery

  1. How do you deliver files to me?

    When you're happy with the result, I'll deploy the final files to a password-protected area on my preview server. This will allow you to review the pages and download the project files at your convenience.

  2. Can you launch my web site for me?

    In WordPress and other CMS projects we can install files on your server as a part of the delivery process.

Payments & Privacy

  1. What about payment process, methods & currencies?

    We'll have to discuss about this in details and we'll choose the best suitable solution that works for both of us.

  2. Can you keep all of my designs & ideas confidential?

    Absolutely. You can trust me. I keep all of your information secret by default. You'll find a Non-Disclosure Agreement(NDA) policy here.

  3. Do I get the rights to do future changes in your code/designs?

    Yes. The rights and ownership of all codes/designs are transferred to you upon payment in full.

I'm as concerned as you are about data security, and that’s why I guarantee that any and all proprietary information you share with me will be held in the strictest confidence.
